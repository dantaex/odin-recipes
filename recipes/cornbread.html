Cornbread

Cornbread recipe. Simple and tasty. Recipes from Budget Bytes Easy to overbake and come out dry.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Grease 8x8 casserole dish.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt until evenly combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and oil.
  4. Pour the bowl of wet ingredients into the bowl of dry ingredients and stir just until everything is moist. Avoid over stirring. Itâ€™s okay if there are a few lumps.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared dish and bake for about 20 minutes, or until the top and edges are golden brown. Cut into 8 pieces and serve.
